Flapcode

Flapcode aims to entertain pupils as they learn mathematics. The idea is similar to that of the popular 2013 smartphone game Flappybird, but we added a twist by using general mathematics. You have 15 seconds to answer the questions and can answer each time you cross three tubes. We choose Flappybird because we believe it's an engaging game that will keep you interested until you complete the level. Playing and studying at the same time will help you become more proficient in mathematics.
